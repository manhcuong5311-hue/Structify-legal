01

Overview

Structify ("we", "our", or "the app") is a personal timeline and habit tracking application. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

The short version: Structify stores all your data locally on your device. We do not have servers that store your personal information. We do not sell, rent, or share your data with anyone.

This Privacy Policy applies to the Structify iOS application and describes how we handle information in connection with your use of our app.

02

Data We Collect

Structify collects minimal data necessary to provide core functionality:

  • Purchase verification — When you purchase Structify Premium, Apple's StoreKit framework verifies your transaction. We receive only a purchase confirmation token, not your payment details.
  • Crash reports — If the app crashes, Apple may collect anonymized crash logs and send them to us via Xcode Organizer. These contain no personally identifiable information.
  • App Store analytics — Apple may provide us with aggregated, anonymous usage statistics (total downloads, country of download). Individual user data is never shared.
03

Data We Do NOT Collect

We want to be explicit about what we do not collect:

  • Your name, email address, or any contact information
  • Your events, habits, schedules, or completion history
  • Your location or GPS data
  • Your device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Biometric data of any kind
  • Health or fitness data beyond what you manually enter
  • Photos, contacts, or any other device content
  • Usage patterns or behavioral analytics

No account required. Structify works entirely without an account. You never need to provide an email or create a profile.

04

Local Storage

All app data — your events, habits, preferences, and completion logs — is stored exclusively on your device using Apple's UserDefaults and local file system.

This data:

  • Never leaves your device to our servers
  • May be included in iCloud backups if you have iCloud backup enabled in iOS Settings (this is controlled entirely by you and Apple)
  • Is permanently deleted when you uninstall the app
  • Can be exported or deleted at any time via the app's Settings → Preferences → Stats & Data section
05

Third-Party Services

Structify uses the following Apple frameworks that may involve data processing:

  • StoreKit 2 — Handles in-app purchases. Subject to Apple's Privacy Policy at apple.com/legal/privacy.
  • UserNotifications — Schedules local notifications. All notification content is generated on-device; nothing is sent to external servers.
  • UIKit / SwiftUI — Standard Apple UI frameworks with no additional data collection.

We do not integrate any third-party analytics SDKs, advertising networks, social media APIs, or tracking libraries.

06

Children's Privacy

Structify does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. Since we collect no personal information from any users, the app is safe for users of all ages.

The app does not contain any age-restricted content, advertising, or in-app social features.

  • Significant changes will be announced within the app on next launch
  • Continued use of Structify after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy

We encourage you to review this page periodically. Previous versions are available upon request.
